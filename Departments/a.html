DESCRIPTION

Accounting specialization is one of the oldest and most prestigious disciplines at the level of universities in the world due to its importance; there is hardly a company or organization, large or small, that there is at least one accountant in it.

Accountancy is the practice of recording, classifying, and reporting on business transactions for a business. It provides feedback to Management, Investors or Government regarding the financial results and status of an organization.

Historically, the concept of accounting has been linked to what the accountant performs in practice, as accounting was viewed as a technical process that includes recording, classifying, and summarizing the malicious operations and drawing the results of these operations.

The concept of accounting has evolved as a result of multiple economic, social, legal, and administrative factors, and accounting has come to be seen as science and art that relies on a set of accepted scientific principles that govern the recording, classification, analysis, and summarization of financial operations.

With the development of the accounting function and the development of the need for accounting information, accounting has come to be seen as a service activity whose primary function is to provide quantitative information of a financial nature mainly on business establishments by assuming that information is used in decision-making.


Accounting branches / departments

Accounting branches diversified and evolved with the diversity and development of the need for accounting information. Below we briefly present the most important of these branches:

  1. Financial Accounting
  2. Cost Accounting
  3. Managerial Accounting
  4. Governmental Accounting
  5. National Accounting
  6. Tax Accounting
  7. Auditing Accounting
