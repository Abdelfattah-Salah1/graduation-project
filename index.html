accounting
Accounting is represented in dealing with financial statements or accounts only. Or Accounting graduates are only qualified To work in banks.
Accounting is represented in many aspects, not just numbers We have the cost accounting which is represented in the cost of raw material in each step in the production process. Or the employees working hours to calculate the payroll.We have management accounting which provide information to support management function specially controlling and evaluation of Performance.Also you can see accounting in designing of the work space or the structure of the office or the production process. This consider one of the controlling techniques because it control the behavior and communication between workers which affect the output. For the layout of production process whether its U-shape or line shape affect the performance of workers. Working in teams instead of working separately (cross function) is one of accounting aspects. So to have controlling system to control the structure of the organization and control the performance measurements consider aspect of accounting.

iT
With the level of technological integration into nearly every facet of life and business, it’s practically essential for companies, large or small, have an Information Technology, or IT, department to handle all the technological issues that arise.Most of us probably don’t know much about the IT department beyond the fact that they’re the ones who occasionally come by to install new software or fix computer-related problems. And while those are among IT’s general responsibilities, that’s just the tip of the iceberg.
The truth is, the IT department does much of their work behind the scenes, and may be much more integral to your company’s success than you realize. • Technical Support The IT department provides this service for all the users who need access to the company's computer systems. This might entail installing new software or hardware, repairing hardware that has become faulty, training employees in the use of new software, and troubleshooting problems with the system or with an individual's computer. • A computer network is a digital telecommunications network for sharing resources between nodes, which are computing devices that use a common telecommunications technology. Data transmission between nodes is supported over data links consisting of physical cable media, such as twisted pair or fiber-optic cables, or by wireless methods, such as Wi-Fi, microwave transmission, or free-space optical communication. Network nodes are network computer devices that originate, route and terminate data communication. • Application developers are in-demand professionals in the information technology market. Application developers are software development professionals who create applications for operating systems or devices, such as smart phones and tablets.

"5ws"

"who"

Graduates who need to prepare for the labour market with sufficient efficiency and effectiveness.

"when"

when senior year students need to know what preparation are made for the labour market from acquiring skills, learn how to prepare their CV, what employment opportunities are available and what courses are needed to strengthen them in various business fields because as we know of course, academic studies at university do not cover the requirements of the labour market, but only open up a door and require the student to complete and strengthen himself in order to be able to cope with the real situation in the labour market.

"where"

as an educational site, this site information is taken from approved sites of courses in the mentioned fields, as well as information from experienced persons who have encountered the labour market and information from other educational sites specializing in mentioned areas. This will be clarified during the site.

"what"

• The designers of this website are senior year students, so we are currently facing this situation. We are in a state of preparation for the labour market. • At this website we tried hard to collect information from various reliable sources in one website that is easily accessible to students and to provide sufficient information for the students about to graduate. • We also enlisted the expertise of the project supervisor, Dr.Mona Gana, in obtaining some reliable sources for research, and the supervisor also supervised all the information we collect to confirm its authenticity. • We also used the experience of some people on the job market to get some information.

"why"

as a senior year students, we know very well how dispersed students are in how to prepare for the labour market. Also the existence of numerous sources causes dispersion for some students and even some students may find it difficult to get proper information on the necessary preparations. So this website may be a way to help students look for the right source of how to prepare for the labour market.
